Steak Frites

Learn how to make this Classic French Steak frites recipe that is served with homemade lemon-herb butter.
This has become one of my all-time favorite steak dishes.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Top Sirloin
  • Avocado Oil
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Start by making your herb butter
  2. Cut the russet potatoes to your desired size and fry them.
  3. Season the steak and add thyme, garlic, and butter, and cook for 3 minutes per side while basting the steak.
  4. Add butter to the top of the steak, slice, and serve with fries
